We aim to build a web application for users who love the outdoors to meet other nature loving individuals. Our alogirthm will be bring individuals who have like minded desires for adventures to a user's screen. At that time, a user will be able to swipe left or right on the individual on the screen and create a life long adventure partner. In the near term, we are creating an application that is attractive to thrill seeking adventures. Stakeholders of this application being users, developers, SCRUM Master, Product Owner, and park managers.
